Italian Pizza

Here's a recipe for a real italian pizza, and everything you need to know make it at home! including a pizza dough tutorial and a step-by-step instructions for it

Want to make a REAL italian pizza at home! for 10 years, Alex and I have been studying the art of home made pizza and now we will show you! Let's make the pizza

Ingridies

  • 1 ball of sour dough pizza
  • 1/3 cup home made pizza sauce
  • 3/4 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 3 tablespoons of basil and pesto
  • Semolina flour for dusting the pizza

How to assemble the piza

  1. rull out the homemade pizza dough
  2. smear the pizza sauce onto the dough
  3. prepare the dough and sauce in ovn for 3min
  4. prepare the cheese
  5. put cheese on the hot pizza
  6. bake the pizza
  7. let the pesto and basil put the final G on the pizza
