there will never be any time too loose learing this recipe and to even make this better, you just need to learn it one time and it should fall in place

Ingridiens

  • Eggs
  • fettuccini
  • Pancetta
  • water
  • parmesan

How to make it!

  1. boil the fettuccini al dente
  2. saute the pancetta and garlic
  3. beat the eggyolks with half of the cheese
  4. toss the pasta with the pancetta
  5. add the beaten egg with the pasta and fettucini
  6. keep moving the mixture with the pasta to make it creamy
  7. add half a saucet skillet of pasta water
  8. ENJOY!!
